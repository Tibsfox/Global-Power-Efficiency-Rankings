## Content Specifications

### Case Studies (75 total)
- 3 per card, detailed paragraphs with measurable outcomes
- Verifiable source URLs (2020-2024 timeframe)
- Focus on established examples with track records

### Project Ideas (25 sets)
- DIY projects readers can build themselves (Python scripts, automations, apps)
- Commercial/startup concepts for the space
- Mix of beginner and advanced complexity

### Career Transition Suggestions (125 total)
Each card gets 5 suggestions:
- **2-3 domain-specific paths** — careers directly related to that card's AI application
- **2-3 transferable AI skills** — broadly applicable skills that empower career changers
- **Vetted resource links** — courses, certifications, communities, tools

**Target audience:** Both workers displaced by AI AND career changers seeking opportunities

**Philosophy:** AI as empowerment tool, not replacement. Help people:
- Leverage existing skills to find efficient study paths
- Become self-sufficient doing work they love
- Manage complexity of modern technology in understandable ways
- Build careers that work alongside AI, not against it
