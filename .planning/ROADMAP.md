# Roadmap: COP28 Energy Efficiency Report v1.2

## Overview

This milestone adds personalized learning paths with AI prompts and self-study resources to all 125 career transition pathways. Each organizational level (Individual through Global) receives 25 pathway enrichments in sequence, followed by comprehensive quality assurance. The structure follows natural organizational boundaries: simpler, hands-on Individual pathways first, building toward complex Global pathways, with QA as the final gate.

## Phase Details

### Phase 6: Individual Level
**Goal**: Career changers at the Individual level can access personalized AI prompts and project ideas for hands-on, home-scale energy efficiency learning
**Depends on**: Nothing (first phase of v1.2)
**Requirements**: IND-01, IND-02, IND-03 (+ LP-01 through LP-05, VAR-01 through VAR-04 apply)
**Success Criteria** (what must be TRUE):
  1. Each of 25 Individual pathways contains an AI prompt example users can copy and adapt for their career transition
  2. AI prompts demonstrate personalized study plan generation with context variables (current role, target role, timeframe)
  3. Each pathway includes 2-3 project portfolio ideas with concrete deliverables spanning beginner to proficient
  4. Learning content emphasizes kinesthetic, hands-on approaches appropriate for home/personal scale
  5. No two adjacent pathways use identical prompt patterns or opening hooks
