Focus on demonstrating how AI enables better policy outcomes and regional coordination.

<decisions>
## Implementation Decisions

### Case Study Selection
- Emphasize regional utilities over state government agencies (ISOs, regional utility consortiums)
- Prioritize geographic diversity across US regions (CAISO, PJM, NYISO, etc.) plus 1-2 international examples (UK/EU)
- Broader timeframe (2019-2024) to include established programs with proven multi-year results
- Prioritize interstate collaboration and multi-state coordination examples
- Balance grid reliability metrics (forecasting accuracy, outage reduction) with economic metrics (cost savings, ROI)
- Avoid politically controversial examples (e.g., ERCOT 2021 crisis framing)
- New cards (Grid Forecasting, AI-Accelerated Permitting): Claude selects most compelling examples with measurable outcomes

### Audience Framing
- Write for both utility planners/grid operators AND policy analysts/regulators equally
- Match technical depth to individual/community levels for consistency
- Emphasize policy impact over operational efficiency
- Explain key grid terminology inline (ISO, load forecasting, demand response)

### Career Path Positioning
- Mix across sectors: utility careers, government/regulatory roles, AND consulting
- Transition paths from both general professional backgrounds (engineers, analysts) AND adjacent energy backgrounds (oil/gas, traditional utilities)
- Include consulting paths (Deloitte, McKinsey energy practice) where natural for topic
- Learning resources: mix of free and paid, with costs clearly noted

### Project Scope
- DIY projects target individuals learning through hands-on practice, not organizational teams
- Use public/open data sources only (EIA, OpenEI, state transparency portals)
- Commercial concepts: both startup scale (SaaS for utilities) and enterprise/B2G scale

### Claude's Discretion
- Specific ISO/utility selections within geographic diversity constraint
- When to include compliance/regulatory opportunities in commercial concepts
- Grid Forecasting and AI-Accelerated Permitting case study selection
- Exact balance of international vs domestic examples

</decisions>
