<decisions>
## Implementation Decisions

### Voice & Authority
- Claude decides tone by pathway (institutional gravitas for policy roles, technical peer for engineering, strategic advisor for program management)
- Mix specific institutions with category explanations (name DOE, NREL, PNNL AND explain what category they represent)
- Claude decides follow-up prompt style by pathway (technical roles get technical follow-ups, policy roles get strategic ones)
- Claude calibrates terminology definitions by pathway (more definitions for career-changer pathways, less for technical expert pathways)

### Federal Program Integration
- BIL/IRA: Light touch — mention as funding context but focus on enduring skills, not current programs
- Project ideas: Mix skills + funding awareness — projects build capabilities with notes on how work connects to federal ecosystem
- DOE offices: Claude decides by relevance — name specific offices (EERE, OE, ARPA-E, LPO) when they're well-known hiring targets
- National labs: Both employers + resources — labs are potential employers AND producers of tools/data/reports career changers can use

### Systems-Scale Framing
- Weave together interconnection focus + impact magnitude + long-term horizons as needed per pathway
- Project progression: Beginner = individually achievable, Proficient = team-scale scenarios showing contribution framing
- Technical depth: Claude calibrates by pathway (engineering pathways get technical, policy pathways get strategic)
- Timeline estimates: Claude decides by project scope — some may fit existing 15-25h/30-50h/50-80h ranges, others may need adjustment

### Prompt Patterns
- Create ALL NEW patterns for National level (5 fresh patterns, no reuse of Phase 8)
- Confirmed pattern themes:
  1. **Program Navigation** — mapping federal programs, funding streams, application pathways
  2. **Cross-Sector Analysis** — understanding energy intersections with transport, buildings, industry
  3. **Infrastructure Lifecycle** — planning, deployment, operations, decommissioning perspectives
  4. **Stakeholder Mapping** — identifying decision-makers, influencers, collaboration opportunities
  5. **Claude develops** — research identifies 5th pattern that best fits the 25 pathways
- Pattern selection: Best-fit per card (Claude picks patterns matching each card's domain, may repeat across cards if fits)

</decisions>

## Specific Ideas

- National labs framed as both career destinations (hiring, fellowships, postdocs) and resource producers (tools, datasets, reports)
- Systems-scale thinking = interconnections + magnitude (GW-scale, millions of customers) + long horizons (2035/2050 targets)
- Projects should show individual contribution to team-scale work at Proficient level
- Keep BIL/IRA as context, not central focus — skills should outlast current funding cycles
